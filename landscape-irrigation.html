Landscape Irrigation

Watering landscapes uniformly is not only important to maintain lush green spaces but also to confirm to the standards of sustainability and Green building that have come into play with Global Water scarcity. Hitech Irrigation has had a legacy of developing landscape for government authorities, corporates, amusement parks to name a few.

Hitech Irrigation offers a complete range of landscape irrigation systems; from the basic manually operated Sprinkler systems to the state of the art 'Automated' systems using 'Pop-Up' Sprinklers/ Spray heads. These advanced landscape irrigation products not only irrigate but beautify the landscape visually by creating a pleasant visual impact. Hitech Irrigation is a complete irrigation solutions provider as we design, supply, install and maintain sustainable irrigation solutions and landscape for the clients on a turnkey basis.
