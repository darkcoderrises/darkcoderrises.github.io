Management

Rajeev Goel

Managing Director

Rajeev Goel has an exhaustive experience in landscape and agriculture irrigation and is an agriculture engineer from GBPU, Pantnagar with specialization in irrigation engineering. Trained in Israel on modern irrigation techniques and designing of irrigation systems, he has 25 years of experience in various practical aspects of green house & irrigation system designing & application.

He has hands on experience in Landscape Irrigation technologies and Vertical Gardens and has delivered various turnkey projects in the landscape domain carefully executing these projects from scratch to finish including Survey, Design, Installation followed by a customer delighting service. A great leader, Mr. Goel has expanded his business into various allied fields under Hitech Irrigations.

K. K. Singh

Landscape Advisor

Prof. K. K. Singh has served the GBPU of Agriculture and Technology, Pantnagar as a Professor and at various administrative positions. He is a B. Tech in Agriculture Engineering from GBPU of Agriculture and Technology, Pantnagar and an M. Tech and PHD from IIT Kharagpur. Having worked on numerous national and international research and irrigation projects, he carries one of the best experience in the country on landscape irrigation.

Devender Sharma

About Hitech Irrigations

Hitech Irrigations started its operations in the landscape irrigation segment since early 1990s with its spread in the Delhi NCR region. Over a period of few years, Hitech Irrigation expanded its reach in the region in the segment of horticulture, fountains, maintenance of landscape projects and is currently an established player in the Delhi NCR region with some exceptional landscape and vertical garden projects of Highways and Municipal corporates.
