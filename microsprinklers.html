About Hitech Irrigations

Hitech Irrigations started its operations in the landscape irrigation segment since early 1990s with its spread in the Delhi NCR region. Over a period of few years, Hitech Irrigation expanded its reach in the region in the segment of horticulture, fountains, maintenance of landscape projects and is currently an established player in the Delhi NCR region with some exceptional landscape and vertical garden projects of Highways and Municipal corporates.
