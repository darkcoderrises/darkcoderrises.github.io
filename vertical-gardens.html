Vertical Gardens

Hitech Irrigations has developed a number of Vertical Gardens across Delhi NCR in both indoor and outdoor space. Green vertical walls can be developed through this. Our experienced team of landscape designers and service professionals can help create beautiful vertical garden designs.

These vertically suspended plants allow you to grow a number of succulent and ornamental plants in a limited space either with hydroponics or cocopeat medium. They act as great air purifier on highways, on metro pillars. In office spaces as well, they can be a great alternative to potted plants which demand more space and greater maintenance, adding a remarkable aesthetic value to the office walls.

About Hitech Irrigations

Hitech Irrigations started its operations in the landscape irrigation segment since early 1990s with its spread in the Delhi NCR region. Over a period of few years, Hitech Irrigation expanded its reach in the region in the segment of horticulture, fountains, maintenance of landscape projects and is currently an established player in the Delhi NCR region with some exceptional landscape and vertical garden projects of Highways and Municipal corporates.
